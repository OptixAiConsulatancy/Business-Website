## Business Overview

- **Business Name**: AI Automation Agency (name TBD)
- **What it does**: Builds and sells AI-powered automated workflows for SMBs who lack in-house skills. Bridges the AI skills gap for businesses.
- **Stage**: Starting out / early stage
- **Location**: Gold Coast, Australia
- **URL**: TBD

## Target Audience

- **Who they are**: Australian SMBs — especially trades & construction, real estate, allied health/medical, hospitality & tourism, professional services (accountants, lawyers, mortgage brokers)
- **Pain points**: Know they should be using AI but have no idea where to start. Lack in-house skills. AI skills gap is the #1 barrier.
- **Goals**: Save time, reduce manual work, automate repetitive processes, cut costs

## Product / Offer

- **Core product or service**: Custom AI automation workflows built with Make, n8n, Zapier + Claude/AI. Consulting, implementation, and ongoing support.
- **Pricing**:
  - Project fee: $1,500–$10,000+ one-off to build
  - Retainer: $300–$2,000/month for hosting, maintenance, improvements
  - Model: Project fee to build + retainer to maintain (recurring revenue)
- **Key differentiator**: Selling clarity and execution, not just technology. Low competition at SMB level vs enterprise.

## What Gets Built (Core Automations)

- Lead follow-up agents — respond to enquiries, qualify leads, book appointments
- Document processing — extract data from invoices/forms, push to spreadsheets/CRMs
- Customer support bots — FAQs, ticket triage, escalation
- Reporting automation — pull data from multiple sources, generate weekly reports
- Onboarding workflows — new client or staff onboarding sequences
- Social/content pipelines — draft content, schedule posts, repurpose across channels

## Business Flow

```
Prospect → Discovery → Diagnose → Build → Deliver → Retain
```

1. **Prospect** — Reach out to businesses in a target niche
2. **Discovery** — Ask questions to understand current processes
3. **Diagnose** — Identify highest-pain, highest-ROI automation opportunities
4. **Build** — Use Claude Code + Make/n8n to build the solution
5. **Deliver** — Deploy, train staff, hand over
6. **Retain** — Ongoing support/maintenance as monthly retainer
